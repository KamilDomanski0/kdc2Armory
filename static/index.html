Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2 Armour & Clothing

Armoury catalogue

Search, filter, and sort armour or clothing pieces stored in the backing SQLite database.

Stats shown are the base values pulled from the game files (full condition, no perk/layer modifiers). The in-game UI may display different numbers once bonuses or wear are applied. Only Henry-wearable armour/clothing is listed (quest props, animal gear, and female-only outfits are excluded).

The catalogue surfaces every wearable entry found in the game's data (including NPC-only garments and quest props), so a few pieces may not rig or animate perfectly on Henry's model. NPC placeholders and quest rewards are hidden by default via the toggles below.
